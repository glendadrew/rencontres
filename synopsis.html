I’ll Fly Away: A Murmuration for Justice

I’ll Fly Away: A Murmuration for Justice presents a display of hope, faith and resoluteness in the face of despair and incarceration.

Three framed prints on rice paper, each featuring a pixelated image generated by our own custom algorithm, highlights the diaristic words of women writing while imprisoned.

Through Augmented Reality, a visual dust storm of pixels rise from the bottom of each print. While the 'pixeldust' swirls in a pattern of the murmuration of birds, an autobiographical statement is heard and displayed, describing hopelessness, despair or fear expressed in that person's life. As the voice/text ends, the pixels assemble and reveal the person's identity, easily recognizable figures who are world renowned for overcoming their despair and making exemplary contributions to our world.
